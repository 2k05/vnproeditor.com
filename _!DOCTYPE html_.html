Best Video Editing Apps in 2024

1. VN Video Editor

Platform: iOS, Android, Windows, macOS

VN Video Editor is a standout choice for both beginners and pros. It has a clean and intuitive interface, offering powerful tools like multi-layer editing, keyframe animations, and color correction. What makes VN even more appealing is that it's free with no watermarks, making it perfect for people looking to create high-quality videos without a hefty price tag.

  • Multi-layer timeline editing
  • Custom keyframe animations
  • Video effects and color correction
  • 4K export with no watermarks
  • Direct sharing to social media

2. Adobe Premiere Rush

Platform: iOS, Android, macOS, Windows

Adobe Premiere Rush is designed for fast and easy editing on the go. As part of the Adobe Creative Cloud family, it syncs seamlessly with Adobe Premiere Pro for more advanced editing if needed. The app provides intuitive drag-and-drop editing, audio mixing, and color grading options.

  • Cross-device syncing (edit on mobile, finish on desktop)
  • Built-in templates for easy social media export
  • Customizable titles, transitions, and effects
  • Easy to export directly to platforms like YouTube and Instagram

3. KineMaster

Platform: iOS, Android

KineMaster is one of the most popular on mobile, loved for its professional features and user-friendly interface. It supports multi-layer video editing, offering precision controls, transitions, and effects. Though it's free, opting for the premium version removes watermarks and provides access to premium features and assets.

  • Multiple video, image, and text layers
  • Blending modes and chroma key (green screen)
  • Instant preview of edits
  • Built-in music, effects, and transitions
  • Supports 4K video exports

4. InShot

Platform: iOS, Android

InShot is a lightweight app that's perfect for quick social media edits. It combines video trimming, merging, and adding background music with basic effects and filters. While it doesn’t have as many advanced features as some other apps, it’s ideal for people who want to quickly edit videos for Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube without a learning curve.

  • Easy video trimming and merging
  • Add music, text, and emojis to videos
  • Video speed control (slow motion or time-lapse)
  • Background blur and crop tool for different aspect ratios
  • Export in HD

5. CapCut

Platform: iOS, Android

CapCut, developed by ByteDance (the creators of TikTok), has gained significant popularity for its seamless integration with TikTok and its impressive range of features. It offers easy-to-use editing tools, effects, and music specifically tailored for short video content, but it also works well for longer, more detailed edits.

  • Built-in music and sound effects library
  • Text and sticker overlays
  • Transition effects and filters
  • Speed adjustment and reverse function
  • Direct export to TikTok with optimized settings

Conclusion

Choosing the right video editing app depends on your needs, whether you're a beginner or a professional content creator. If you're a beginner, start with VN Video Editor or InShot for ease of use. For more advanced editing, go with KineMaster or Adobe Premiere Rush. Each of these apps brings something unique to the table, so find the one that fits your workflow and start creating amazing videos today!
